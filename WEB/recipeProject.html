Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bread (white/milk bread)
  • 2 tbsp milk (or as needed)
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • Oil/ghee for frying

For Sugar Syrup:

  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 cardamom pods (crushed)

Recipe

  1. Prepare Sugar Syrup: Boil sugar + water until slightly sticky. Add cardamom and keep warm.
  2. Make Dough: Cut bread crusts, crumble bread finely. Add milk and knead into soft dough.
  3. Shape & Fry: Roll into small balls. Fry in low-heat oil until golden brown.
  4. Soak in Syrup: Dip fried balls in warm syrup for 30 minutes.

Coconut Burfi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated coconut (fresh or desiccated)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup milk
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 4 cardamom pods (crushed)
  • Chopped nuts (optional)

Recipe

  1. Cook Coconut & Sugar: In a pan, mix coconut + sugar + milk. Cook on medium heat, stirring until sugar dissolves.
  2. Thicken the Mixture: Add ghee and keep stirring until it thickens (starts leaving the pan's sides). Add cardamom and nuts.
  3. Set in Tray: Pour mixture into a greased tray and flatten evenly.
  4. Cool & Cut: Let it cool for 1 hour, then cut into squares.
